3. The returnee signal and diversity flag are both inferred from imperfect proxies. Name the two most likely failure modes in your implementation — where will it confidently misclassify? What is the right human-in-the-loop step before a recruiter acts on these flags?

First, returnee misclassification: candidates studying/working abroad briefly may be incorrectly flagged as returnees. Second, diversity proxy errors: name-based or heuristic inference can misclassify gender or background.
Both errors are high-confidence but wrong due to weak proxies. A human review step should validate flagged attributes before recruiter decisions—especially for sensitive attributes like diversity.
